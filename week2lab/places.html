My Favorite Places

Ireland

It has such an epic mountain peaks, magnificent coastlines, lofty lighthouses and rich greenery that I would love to go for a vacation.

New Zealand

It is famous for its national rugby team, its indigenous Maori culture and its picturesque landscape.

Australia

Australia is world famous for its natural wonders and wide open spaces, its beaches, deserts, "the bush", and "the Outback".

Turkey

With crystal clear waters, fantastic beaches, and quaint fishing villages, the Turkish coast is one of the most pristine in the world.

Japan

Japan is special for its architecture, art, traditions, and crafts.

Paris

It is nicknamed as the "City of light" and also is the capital city of France.
